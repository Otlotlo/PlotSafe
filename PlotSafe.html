How PlotSafe works

PlotSafe is a trusted intermediary that verifies land ownership and secures payments via escrow. Every plot is checked with the Department of Landboard before listing. Buyers pay into an escrow account; funds are released only after the title is transferred.

Frequently asked questions

How long does verification take? Usually 2-3 business days.

What if the seller is fraudulent? We verify ownership, so fraud is prevented.

Is my money safe? Yes, funds are held in a regulated escrow account.
